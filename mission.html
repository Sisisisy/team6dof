Mission Strategy

Reliable first. Then ambitious.

Waypoint Endurance

Autonomous waypoint navigation is prioritized to ensure stable flight, endurance, and mission readiness before entering advanced tasks.

Search → Detect → Deliver

The mission is structured as a complete pipeline where perception, localization, and payload delivery work together as a unified system.

Operator Efficiency

The system is designed to reduce operator workload while maintaining visibility, safety, and control during mission execution.

What We Prioritize

• Strong subsystem ownership
• Repeatable mission flow
• Real testing over assumptions
• Clear operator awareness
• Honest and accurate documentation

Trade-offs

• Conservative autonomy decisions
• Complexity only if tested
• Integration cost matters
• Mission success over flashy features
• Time spent on testing vs adding features

Strategy Philosophy

The mission strategy focuses on what can be reliably executed under real competition conditions. Every added feature must survive integration, testing, and field validation. The goal is not to overcomplicate the system, but to deliver a clean, stable, and competitive solution.
