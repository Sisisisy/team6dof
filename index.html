Our Mission

Built from scratch.
Designed to win.

Every bolt, line of code, and flight path is engineered by our team at KFUPM — zero off-the-shelf shortcuts.

Full-Stack Autonomy

From Pixhawk-based flight control to Jetson-powered computer vision, our stack is fully integrated end-to-end. No black boxes, no shortcuts — just engineering.

AI / Perception

Real-time object detection and target classification running on-board the drone, built with our custom AI pipeline and computer vision stack.

Precision Payload

Custom payload bay with autonomous drop mechanism engineered for sub-meter accuracy under full competition conditions.

Architecture

Seven subteams.
One mission.

Every discipline covered by a dedicated team — tightly integrated under one systems lead.

01 — AIRFRAME + PAYLOAD

Airframe & Payload

Structural design, mechanical integration, and autonomous payload deployment — from the drone body to sub-meter drop accuracy.

02 — GN&C

GN&C

Guidance, navigation, and control algorithms using Pixhawk 6X with RTK GPS for centimeter-level positioning accuracy.

03 — AI / PERCEPTION

AI / Perception

Computer vision pipeline for real-time target detection and classification powered by Jetson Nano onboard processing.

04 — AVIONICS

Avionics

Electronics, power distribution, custom PCB design, and full sensor integration across the entire vehicle stack.

05 — WEBSITE

Website

Design and development of the team's web presence — documenting progress, showcasing systems, and communicating the mission.

06 — MEDIA

Media

Photography, videography, and content creation capturing every milestone, lab session, and flight test throughout the season.

07 — LOGISTICS

Logistics

Procurement, scheduling, budget management, and competition coordination — keeping the whole team moving forward.
