About the Project

This homepage gives a quick overview of every major part of the project and connects to the detailed pages.

Our focus is end-to-end UAV system integration: from perception and decision-making, to airframe design, avionics, mission execution, and competition deliverables.

Instead of treating subsystems separately, we build around one goal: a reliable mission-ready vehicle that can be tested, explained, and improved as one complete system.
