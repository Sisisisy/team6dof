    <div class="eyebrow">AI and Autonomy</div>
    <h2 class="sh">From pixels to decisions.</h2>
    <p class="slead">Perception is designed to support the actual scoring tasks, not just produce detections. The pipeline combines onboard imagery, filtering, localization, and mission logic so that detections can translate into usable mapping outputs and delivery decisions.</p>
    </div>
    <div class="pl reveal scale-in">
    <div class="ps"><div class="psn">01</div><div class="pst">Image Capture</div><div class="psd">A mission camera captures frames over the search boundary and mission area.</div></div>
    <div class="ps"><div class="psn">02</div><div class="pst">Preprocessing</div><div class="psd">Frames are prepared for inference and tagged with pose data for downstream mapping and localization.</div></div>
    <div class="ps"><div class="psn">03</div><div class="pst">Detection</div><div class="psd">Targets of interest are detected and filtered to reduce false positives before any action is taken.</div></div>
    <div class="ps"><div class="psn">04</div><div class="pst">Localization</div><div class="psd">Pixel-space detections are converted into usable world positions using altitude, heading, and camera geometry.</div></div>
    <div class="ps"><div class="psn">05</div><div class="pst">Decision Output</div><div class="psd">Targets are passed to mission logic for review, re-survey, mapping output, or payload delivery planning.</div></div>

    <div class="reveal from-left">
    <div class="eyebrow">Detection Logic</div>
    <h3 style="font-family:var(--fd);font-weight:700;font-size:1.15rem;margin-bottom:0.9rem;letter-spacing:-0.02em">Confidence before action</h3>
    <p style="font-size:0.875rem;color:var(--muted);margin-bottom:1rem;line-height:1.75">Detections are filtered before they become mission actions. The system favors confidence, repeat observations, and operator awareness over risky one-shot decisions.</p>
    <p style="font-size:0.875rem;color:var(--muted);line-height:1.75">That lets the team preserve reliability while still making use of AI for mission scoring.</p>
    </div>
    <div class="reveal from-right">
    <div class="eyebrow">Mapping Output</div>

    <p style="font-size:0.875rem;color:var(--muted);line-height:1.75">The same perception stack also supports delivery target identification and localization.</p>
