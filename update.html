March 29, 2026 Lab Session

From 3 to 11 — From Idea to Action 🚀

On Sunday, March 29, Team 6-DOF reached a major milestone: our very first lab session together.

During this session, we successfully set up the Pixhawk, connected it to the RC system, and achieved full actuation control. We then integrated the system with the ground station and took it a step further by developing our own code from scratch on the Jetson Nano companion computer.

What began as a small group of three driven individuals has now grown into a team of eleven — each member bringing unique skills, energy, and vision.

This lab was more than just hardware and code. It was about collaboration, problem-solving, and building something bigger than ourselves.

This is only the beginning.
