Requirements

SUAS 2026 compliance.

Every mandatory requirement from the SUAS 2026 handbook — verified.

Max AUW ≤ 35 lbs

All-up weight target 14 kg (~30.9 lbs) — 4.1 lbs below the hard limit. Verified at safety inspection.

Autonomous RTH / RTL

ArduPilot failsafe enables auto Return-to-Home. Both Safety Pilot and GCS Operator can trigger it independently.

Autonomous Flight Termination

Configurable flight termination via Pixhawk failsafe. Independently activatable by Safety Pilot and GCS Operator.

FAA Remote ID

Compliant Remote ID broadcast including unique vehicle ID and real-time position. FAA DroneZone registered.

No Ground Tethers

Free-flying heavier-than-air platform. No tethers, ground encumbrances, or dependency on public cloud systems.

No Parts Depart in Flight

Shear-designed propellers stay on aircraft. Payload drops only during designated delivery task attempts.

FCC-Compliant RF

All RF systems (RFD900ux telemetry, WiFi, RC link) operate within FCC-allowed bands. Encryption enabled.

Onboard Imaging Payload

SIYI A8 Mini gimbal + Arducam IMX219 are airborne sensors. No ground-based imaging used as a substitute.
